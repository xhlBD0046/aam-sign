Channel Letters Applications

Custom channel letters are a great way to establish brand reputation and raise visibility both indoors and outdoors. From taking the spotlight at trade show displays to creating an eye-catching storefront, custom channel letters are a sure choice.

Get creative with your custom channel letters and display your logo sign at your store facade, in your reception area, or elsewhere.

Indoor Channel Letters

Add a professional edge to your brand by lighting up your indoor space with a backlit acrylic sign. If you need office lobby signs featuring your brand name or logo, channel letter signs are a radiant solution. They will transform your entire office into a more inviting space.

Outdoor Channel Letters

Channel letter signs will distinguish your brand in crowded business centers. These building signs are attention-grabbing and merge well with the facade of any building. Custom channel letters, like backlit logo signs, foster brand familiarity each time customers see them.

Channel Letters FAQs

Q. What type of illumination do you use for custom channel letters?
A.
For custom channel letters, we use energy-efficient LED strips. They're attached to the interior of returns, allowing light to emit through a translucent lexan face. LED channel letters are more cost-effective, eco-friendly, and last longer than displays with incandescent bulbs.
Q. What materials are used for making channel letter signs?
A.
Typically, channel letter signs are made of aluminum and lexan materials. However, we can customize the product to fully meet your needs and preferences.
Q. What are the illumination options for channel letter signs?
A.
Front-lit channel letters are one of the most widespread varieties. Custom channel letters with side, back, and double illumination are also highly sought-after across the industry. You're free to go for more unique designs as we offer individualized options, too.
Q. What mounting options are available for custom channel letter signs?
A.
The mounting options vary depending on your specifications. You can order custom channel letters on raceway or wireway for housing all the electrical components. LED channel letters can also be flush-mounted, freestanding, or hanging. You may send us your special mounting requirements for consideration.
